Government Schemes & Support

National Level Schemes

Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan

Encourages local entrepreneurship in urban agriculture, building food sovereignty and local resilience.

National Urban Livelihoods Mission (NULM)

Promotes self-employment and micro-enterprise development among the urban poor engaged in agricultural activities.

Startup India

Promotes agri-tech startups like hydroponics, rooftop farming, and indoor farm technologies.

PM Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PM-FME)

Facilitates micro food processing units to scale up urban farm-to-market initiatives.

Swachh Bharat Mission - Urban

Encourages community-scale composting as part of solid waste management goals, with focus on farm-to-waste direct linkages.
